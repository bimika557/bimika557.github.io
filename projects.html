My Projects

Solar powered Mars Rover

I designed and developed a Bluetooth-controlled Mars Rover Car featuring a rocker-bogie suspension system, inspired by NASA's planetary rovers. This project aimed to create a vehicle capable of navigating rough and uneven terrains while maintaining stability and traction.

Sushtho Neer

I actively participated in a social changemakers project under the Amra Notun Network of BRAC, dedicated to improving the health and well-being of underprivileged women and children suffering from anemia. Our mission was to create a meaningful impact by addressing nutritional deficiencies and raising awareness about preventive healthcare.
